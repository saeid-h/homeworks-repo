Chapter Aims
  • To problematize media representations of crime and criminal victimization.
  • To discuss methods of researching criminal victimization and media coverage.
  • To assess media representations of different forms of criminal victimization.
Introduction

The definition of who may legitimately claim victim status is profoundly influenced by social divisions including class, race, ethnicity, gender, age and sexuality, and, as such, remains a point of contention and debate.
